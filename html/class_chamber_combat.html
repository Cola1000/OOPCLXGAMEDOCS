Detailed Description

Represents a combat-focused chamber inside a dungeon.

Handles the logic for generating mobs, calculating rewards, penalties, and item bonuses. The chamber generates random mobs within specified level ranges, difficulty scales, and calculates appropriate rewards or penalties based on the outcome of combat.
